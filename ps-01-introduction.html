01. Introduction to Professional Skills

Introduction to Professional Skills

Foundation module covering employability skills, workplace competencies, and the Johari Window framework for personal development.

Core Employability Skills

Communication

Clear, impactful, and adaptive.

Teamwork

Collaboration that creates results.

Problem-Solving

Turning challenges into opportunities.

Adaptability

Flexible in change, steady in purpose.

Leadership

Guiding with vision and empathy.

The Johari Window Framework

Visual framework to understand conscious and unconscious biases

The Johari Window provides a visual representation of self-awareness, helping individuals understand how they perceive themselves versus how others perceive them.

Self-Awareness

Understand your strengths and blind spots to improve personal effectiveness and professional relationships.

Personal Development

Identify areas for improvement and growth in professional settings through increased self-awareness.

Better Relationships

Build stronger workplace connections through improved understanding of yourself and others.

Workplace Skills

Technical Skills

Specific expertise and knowledge required for a particular job or industry. These are the hard skills that demonstrate your ability to perform specific tasks and use particular tools or technologies.

Soft Skills

Interpersonal and communication skills that enhance overall work performance and collaboration. These include emotional intelligence, teamwork, and adaptability.

Transferable Skills

Versatile skills that can be applied across various roles and industries, such as problem-solving, critical thinking, and adaptability. These skills make you valuable in any professional context.
