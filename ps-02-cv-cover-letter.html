Cover Letter Writing

Cover Letter Structure

1

Header & Greeting

Include contact information and address the letter to the hiring manager by name when possible.

2

Opening Paragraph

State the position applying for and how learned about the opportunity. Grab attention with a strong opening.

3

Body Paragraphs

Explain why interested in the role and company. Highlight relevant experience and achievements that match the job requirements.

4

Closing Paragraph

Express enthusiasm for the opportunity, request an interview, and thank the reader for their consideration.

5

Professional Closing

End with "Sincerely" or "Best regards" followed by full name and signature.
