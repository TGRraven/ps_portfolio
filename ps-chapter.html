What are Professional Skills (PS) Chapters?

Professional Skills, also known as soft skills or employability skills, are the non-technical, interpersonal abilities that are essential for success in the workplace. These skills complement technical expertise and play a crucial role in career advancement, team collaboration, and overall professional effectiveness.

Communication Skills

Develop effective verbal and written communication abilities, including presentation skills, active listening, and professional correspondence.

Leadership & Teamwork

Build leadership capabilities and learn to work collaboratively in teams, manage conflicts, and inspire others to achieve common goals.

Critical Thinking

Enhance analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and decision-making skills essential for professional success and innovation.
